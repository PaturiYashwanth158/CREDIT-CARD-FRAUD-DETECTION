    Control Protocol

    How this first version behaves

    01

    Arm deliberately

    An open palm held steady turns the assistant on. A fist held steady shuts everything down immediately.

    02

    Move with intention

    Pointing enters air-mouse mode. The index fingertip becomes a cursor target across your screen.

    03

    Use short commands

    Peace toggles playback, thumbs adjust volume, palm swipes handle track changes and desktop views.
